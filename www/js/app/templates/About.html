About Us

Atlantic Tele-Network, Inc. (ATN) is a telecommunications company operating advanced wireless, wireline, and both terrestrial and submarine fiber optic networks in North America and the Caribbean.

Our expertise is operating in underserved or niche markets, where we can develop a leadership position. We have grown our business organically and through acquisitions by:

  • providing an attractive alternative to larger carriers,
  • offering differentiated services that satisfy the needs of underserved customers,
  • making significant, ongoing investments in network quality and capability, and;
  • giving the management of our acquired companies the freedom to operate independently and creatively.

We are proud of our track record of delivering very strong financial results since going public in 1991, consistently paying dividends, while growing both our business and shareholder value.
